Life's greatest lessons are learnt on the games field and sports create an equal opportunity to be different. At Bhoroli, Physical Education is an integral and daily part of the School routine. Bhoroli has a strong reputation for sport and there is a demanding and growing fixture list, with depth in teams.

The Bhorolites exerted themselves in fields more than one and have indeed done the house proud, at the same time encouraging the house to reach higher targets in the coming years. The `Bhoroli girls take to their interests and choose from the plethora of sports catered to them. It is not only an exhilarating learning experience for them but also an opportunity to develop what they already harnessed.

The biggest achievement the House achieved in the field of Sports is quite possibly the Sports Cup, 2015.

 

 

 

This perennial virtue of hard work and achievement that has imbedded itself in the minds of the Bhorolites and continues to flow and flourish.
